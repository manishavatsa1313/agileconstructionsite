ABOUT US

Agile Pre-engineered Buildings & Structures, is an organization that believes in customer relationship, best solutions and on-time delivery. We listen to our customer about their requirements and come up with solutions. These solutions can then be designed, built and delivered to the customer. Quality you deserve, Timely deliveries that you can count on, Dependability you can vouch for.

Integrated Software Technology helps in managing the project and provide efficient service.

Infrastructure

Agile is equipped to deliver a world class building with the support of:

  • The state-of-art manufacturing facility at Kadapa, Andhra Pradesh.
  • PEB production capacity of 25,000 MT per annum (planned to scale up to 50,000 MT).
  • Heavy structural fabrication capacity of 24,000 MT per annum (planned to scale up to 34,000 MT).
  • Support of the fully integrated software (complete integration of design, engineering, manufacturing and logistics).
  • Fully trained technical manpower.
  • Growing expertise in ‘eco-friendly’ buildings.
  • Continuous improvement through customer feedback
